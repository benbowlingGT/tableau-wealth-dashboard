# Wealth Management Performance Dashboard (Tableau)

## Overview

This project is a Tableau dashboard built to analyze wealth advisor performance across key business metrics including revenue, client growth, and assets under management (AUM).

The goal was to create a clean, executive-level view that highlights performance vs. targets without overwhelming stakeholders. The focus is on clarity, trend visibility, and delivering insights that can actually drive decisions.

This project reflects real-world financial services reporting and how leadership teams evaluate performance across advisors over time.

---

##  Business Problem

Leadership teams need a fast and clear way to understand:

* Which advisors are meeting or missing revenue targets
* How client growth is trending over time
* Where performance gaps exist across the team

Traditional reporting can often be cluttered or overly negative (e.g., too much red). This dashboard was designed to simplify the message and present performance in a more balanced, actionable way.

---

##  Key Metrics

* Total Revenue (Monthly + Trend)
* % to Target
* Net Client Growth (6-Month View)
* Assets Under Management (AUM)
* Variance (Actual vs Target)

Each KPI is designed to show both current performance and direction.

---

##  Dashboard Features

* KPI cards with directional indicators (↑ ↓)
* Monthly revenue and performance trend analysis
* Advisor-level performance comparison
* Heatmap showing monthly performance by advisor
* Clean color strategy to reduce negative bias


---

##  Tools & Data

**Tools Used:**

* Tableau (Dashboard development)
* Excel (Data preparation)

**Dataset:**

* Mock financial dataset (Jan–Jun performance)

**Key Fields:**

* Advisor Name
* Monthly Revenue
* Target Revenue
* New Clients / Lost Clients
* Assets Under Management (AUM)

---



 ##  Key Insights
- Revenue performance alone did not fully reflect advisor effectiveness, client growth trends revealed potential long-term risks  
- Advisor performance was inconsistent month-over-month, indicating volatility rather than steady growth  
- Variance between actual and target required additional context, as large differences were not always indicative of poor performance  

---

##  Future Improvements

* Add regional or segment-level breakdowns
* Introduce revenue forecasting
* Build drill-through views for advisor-level deep dives
* Automate data refresh for near real-time reporting
